Good fit for this drive. Free-space overwrite works as intended on traditional hard drives. About 1.2 TB will be written across 3 passes.
This does not erase current files. It writes temporary data into free space, then removes it. Avoid running it while the drive is busy.
Cancel asks for confirmation and reports that the drive was only partially processed. Cleanup attempts to remove any temporary cipher files.
