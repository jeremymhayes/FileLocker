# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep discussions professional, focused, and constructive.

Good participation includes:

- Using respectful language.
- Staying on topic.
- Assuming good intent while still being precise about technical issues.
- Giving actionable feedback.
- Respecting privacy and not posting sensitive data.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Posting private information without permission.
- Publishing exploit details for unresolved vulnerabilities outside the security reporting process.
- Repeated off-topic comments or spam.
- Dismissing safety, privacy, or data-loss concerns without technical reasoning.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments and may restrict participation when behavior disrupts the project or puts users at risk.

Security reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
