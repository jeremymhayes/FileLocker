# FileLocker 1.2.1.0

FileLocker 1.2.1.0 focuses on clearer System Care workflows and a smoother elevated browsing experience.

## What's New

- Refreshed Startup Manager, App Manager, and Registry Fixer with clearer tables, details panels, and safer action flows.
- Rebuilt Custom Clean into a user-facing cleanup screen with real scan results, safety labels, filters, and select-all controls.
- Expanded Custom Clean coverage across Windows, browsers, applications, gaming tools, developer caches, privacy traces, and advanced cleanup areas.
- Added graceful unavailable and not-found states so unsupported cleanup areas do not look like failures.
- Improved Browse Files and Browse Folder behavior when FileLocker is running as administrator.

## Safety Notes

- Cookies, browser history, developer caches, advanced cleanup, and other review-sensitive items are not selected by default.
- Advanced cleanup areas remain review-first and may require administrator access.
- Unsupported cleanup items are shown as unavailable instead of being guessed from unsafe paths.
